# Community & Tone3000

Soundshed Guitar connects to two online services so you can explore and download amp models and presets created by the community.

---

## Tone3000 — Amp model library

**Tone3000** is an online library of Neural Amp Models contributed by the community. You can browse thousands of captures of real amplifiers and download any of them directly into your library.

### Browsing models

1. Open the **Community** tab in the navigation bar.
2. Select the **Models** section.
3. Browse by category (e.g. British, American, High Gain, Clean) or use the search bar to find a specific amp name.
4. Click a model to see its details — the amp it captures, who made the capture, and user ratings.

### Downloading a model

1. Click **Download** on any model listing.
2. The model is saved to your local library and is immediately available in the resource picker inside the Signal Chain Editor.

### Authentication

Some features (ratings, downloading premium content) require a Tone3000 account:

1. Open **Settings → Tone3000 API Key**.
2. Paste your API key from your Tone3000 account dashboard.
3. Click **Save**. Your account is now linked.

## Soundshed Community — Preset sharing

The Soundshed Community lets you browse and download complete presets — full signal chains with amp models, cabinets, and effects configured — shared by other players.

### Browsing community presets

1. Open the **Community** tab and select **Presets**.
2. Browse by category, genre, or search by name.
3. Click a preset to preview its signal chain and description.

### Downloading a community preset

1. Click **Download** on a preset listing.
2. The preset is added to your **Community** folder in the Preset Browser and loaded automatically.
3. Any models or IRs referenced by the preset are downloaded alongside it.

### Sharing your own presets

1. In the Preset Browser, right-click a preset and choose **Share with Community**.
2. Add a description, tags, and choose a category.
3. Click **Publish**. Your preset is visible to other users on the Soundshed platform.

## Creating a NAM Blend

The **Blend Editor** lets you mix two or more NAM models together to create a hybrid tone:

1. In the resource browser, click **New Blend**.
2. Add two or more models to the blend.
3. Adjust the mix percentage for each model using the sliders.
4. Save the blend — it appears in your library as a regular model and can be loaded into any NAM Amp node.

Snap mode locks blend ratios to whole percentages; Interpolate mode allows smooth fractional mixing.
